How Tindahan 404 Works for Your Store

1

Owner's Central Control

The store owner logs in to the main administrative panel, the heart of Tindahan 404. From here, they can set up the store's initial inventory, manage product details, and crucially, create and manage user accounts for their team.

(Admin Login)
2

Inventory Manager's Hub

Designated inventory managers access their dedicated panel. Here, they can efficiently add new stock, update quantities, track incoming and outgoing products, and monitor real-time inventory levels to prevent 'Not Found' scenarios.

(Login > Inventory Panel)
3

Cashier's Point-of-Sale

Cashiers use their specific login to access the streamlined Point-of-Sale (POS) interface. They can quickly process customer purchases, scan items, apply discounts, and handle transactions, all while inventory updates seamlessly in the background.

(Login > POS)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Tindahan 404 suitable for small Sari-Sari stores?

Absolutely! Tindahan 404 is specifically designed to meet the unique needs of Filipino Sari-Sari stores, from small neighborhood shops to slightly larger operations, making inventory and sales management simple.

Can multiple users access the system at the same time?

Yes, Tindahan 404 supports multiple user roles. The owner can create separate accounts for Inventory Managers and Cashiers, allowing them to access their respective panels simultaneously for efficient operations.

Is the system accessible on different devices?

Tindahan 404 is a web-based system, meaning it can be accessed from any device with a modern web browser and internet connection, including desktops, laptops, and tablets.

How does Tindahan 404 help prevent 'Not Found' items?

By providing real-time inventory tracking, alerts for low stock, and efficient stock management tools for inventory managers, the system helps you maintain accurate records and ensure products are always available when customers need them.
